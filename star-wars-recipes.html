Baby Yoda Blue Cookies Recipe

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 1 Hungry Child
Ingredients Quantity
Space Cookie Dough 30lbs
Bantha Milk 5liters

Step 1: Mix the Bantha milk and the space cookie dough in a bowl

Step 2: Add a pinch of The Force, spoon into cupcake tray

Step 3: Cook on full Jedi heat for 90 mins

Step 4: Make sure Grogu waits for the cookies to cool down before eating

Source: Own recipe

Bantha Milkshake

  • Time: 4 hours 10 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Blue Bantha Milk 5ltrs

Step 1: Find willing Bantha.

Step 2: Milk Bantha into recipient.

Step 3: Pasturize milk with heat from lightsaber

Step 4: Let milk chill for 4 hours and enjoy

Source: Jedi Legend Recipe

Sith Lord Pizza

  • Time: 60mins
  • Serves: 2 Hungry Jedi
Ingredients Quantity
Jedi Essence 50 souls
pizza dough 5lbs
mushrooms 3 kilos

Step 1: Bespin pizza dough at end of lightsaber.

Step 2: Heat oven to lava degrees

Step 3: chop mushrooms

Step 4: prepare souls

Step 5: levitate pizza into extremely hot oven

Step 6: cut with novelty Ewok pizza cutter and enjoy

Source: Jedi Legend Recipe
